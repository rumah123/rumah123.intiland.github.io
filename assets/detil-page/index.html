A delightfully green boutique property development in the tranquil south of Jakarta.

An unusually large, green complex.
Homeowners will appreciate the careful attention to detail and design, as well as the good quality construction that makes the most of the simple materials used. The materials selected are deliberately basic for easy maintenance. This unusually large 4 hectare is lined with pocket gardens and footpaths for jogging. There’s plenty of room to relax and breathe here. Four leading architects – Andra Matin, Antony Liu, Gregorius Supie, and Ridwan Kamil – worked in synergy to create Ozone.

Kawasan Premium

Residents of Ozone have the best of both worlds: they have good access to nearby multiple major traffic networks, while enjoying peace and tranquility inside the compound itself.

Ramah Lingkungan

The numerous facilities are built into the flow of the compound, enhancing its neighborhood community feel.
